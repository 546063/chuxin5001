## 2. 文案要先讲客户，不要先讲自己

很多机械类文案容易这样写：

“We are professional manufacturer.”
“We have high quality machine.”
“We have many years experience.”

这些话不是不能用，但不应该放在最前面。  
客户最关心的不是我是谁，而是“这和我有什么关系”。

更好的表达：

- “Need to reduce manual vegetable cutting labor?”
- “Looking for a machine for potato and onion processing?”
- “Want stable cutting size for your food factory?”
- “Not sure which meat cutter is suitable for frozen meat?”
- “Buying food processing machines from China? Check this before ordering.”

先讲客户问题，客户才愿意继续看我是谁。

---

## 3. 文案要卖结果，不只卖功能

功能是机器能做什么。  
结果是客户得到什么。

功能表达：

“This machine can cut potato.”

结果表达：

“This machine helps small food factories cut potato faster and more evenly.”

功能表达：

“Stainless steel machine.”

结果表达：

“Stainless steel structure makes cleaning easier for food processing use.”

功能表达：

“500kg/h capacity.”

结果表达：

“Suitable for small factories needing stable 500kg/h production.”

客户不是为功能付款，而是为结果付款。

---

## 4. 文案要具体，不要空泛

空泛文案：

- High quality
- Best price
- Good service
- Professional factory
- Advanced machine

这些词客户看太多了，信任感很低。

具体文案：

- 300–800kg/h capacity options
- Suitable for potato, onion, carrot, cabbage
- Testing video before delivery
- Wooden case packing
- Spare parts support
- 220V/380V customizable
- For restaurant, central kitchen, and small food factory
- Export experience to Middle East and Southeast Asia

具体信息越多，客户越容易相信。

---

## 5. 文案必须有明确行动

内容再好，如果没有行动号召，客户可能看完就走。

常见错误 CTA：

“Contact us.”
“Welcome inquiry.”
“DM me.”

更好的 CTA：

- “Send your material + capacity, I will suggest the right model.”
- “Comment your product, I will tell you which machine is suitable.”
- “DM ‘potato’ to get testing video.”
- “Tell me your country and voltage for quotation.”
- “Need 300kg/h or 800kg/h? Message me for comparison.”

行动越具体，客户越容易回复。

## 三、这本书如何帮我做出让客户主动找我的内容？

《文案圣经》能帮我把内容从“机械展示”改成“客户问题解决”。

客户主动找我，通常不是因为我说“我有好机器”，而是因为我的文案让他产生：

- 这个问题我也有
- 这个机器可能适合我
- 这个供应商看起来懂行
- 我可以先问问型号
- 我需要知道价格和运费
- 我想看看测试视频
- 我想发给老板确认

所以，我的文案要围绕客户决策过程写，而不是围绕产品目录写。

适合我的文案方向：

- 帮客户选型
- 帮客户避坑
- 帮客户判断产能
- 帮客户理解价格差异
- 帮客户降低采购风险
- 帮客户确认机器是否适合本地市场
- 帮客户比较人工和机器成本
- 帮客户看到真实交付证据

## 四、直接应用场景

## 1. 视频开头3秒怎么用这本书的理论设计？

视频开头3秒要使用强利益或强问题。

## 问题型开头

- “Still cutting vegetables manually?”
- “Why does your cutting size look uneven?”
- “Not sure which vegetable cutter to buy?”
- “Is cheap machine really cheaper?”
- “Buying from China? Check the testing video first.”

## 结果型开头

- “Cut potato, onion, and carrot faster with one machine.”
- “For small food factories needing stable cutting size.”
- “One machine helps reduce manual cutting work.”
- “Before delivery, we test the real cutting effect.”
- “This model is suitable for 300–800kg/h production.”

## 避坑型开头

- “Don’t buy a cutter before confirming your material.”
- “Don’t compare price before checking capacity.”
- “Wrong blade means poor cutting result.”
- “Low price may cost more if the machine is not suitable.”
- “Check voltage before ordering food machinery.”

这些开头比直接说机器名称更能抓住客户。

---

## 2. 评论区回复怎么用？

评论区回复要简短、具体、专业，并引导下一步。

客户问：“price?”

回复：

“Price depends on material and capacity. Please tell me what you process and how many kg/h you need, then I can suggest the correct model.”

客户问：“Can cut meat?”

回复：

“This model is for vegetables. For fresh meat or frozen meat, you need a different machine structure. I can send the correct option.”

客户问：“Need for small factory.”

回复：

“For small factory, we need to confirm your material, capacity, and working hours. A medium model may be more cost-effective than the biggest one.”

客户问：“Ship to Saudi?”

回复：

“Yes, we can arrange shipping. Please confirm your city/port and voltage, then I can check the packing and delivery solution.”

评论区回复的作用是公开展示专业感，同时把客户引到私信。

## 3. 主页 Bio/简介怎么写？

基于《文案圣经》，主页简介要清晰说明：

1. 我卖什么
2. 我帮谁
3. 我解决什么问题
4. 客户下一步该怎么做

```

## Bio版本3：转化型更强

```

Bio不要写得太虚，必须让客户一眼知道你能帮他做什么。

## 4. 私信第一句话怎么写？

私信第一句话要做到：

- 感谢客户
- 不废话
- 立刻确认需求
- 引导客户回复

## 客户问价格

“Thanks for your inquiry. I can send the price. To suggest the correct model, please tell me your material and required capacity per hour.”

## 客户问机器详情

“Sure. This machine has different models for different materials and capacities. What product do you process, and is it for restaurant or factory use?”

## 客户从视频来

“Thanks for watching the video. Do you need this machine for potato, onion, cabbage, meat, or another material?”

## 客户问能否发货

“Yes, we can arrange shipping. Please tell me your country, city/port, and machine model or required capacity. Then I can check the solution.”

第一句话不要太长，不要一次性塞太多信息。目标是让客户回复。

## 五、结合食品机械/工业设备的1个内容创作公式

## 公式：客户问题 + 具体结果 + 证明材料 + 明确行动

```text
你是否遇到这个问题？
+
这台机器能带来什么具体结果
+
给你看真实测试/案例/出货证明
+
发送你的原料和产能，我给你推荐型号
```

## 示例脚本

```text
Still cutting vegetables manually in your food factory?

This vegetable cutting machine can process potato, onion, carrot, and cabbage with stable cutting size.

Before delivery, we test the machine with real materials and send the video to the customer.

If you need 300kg/h, 500kg/h, or 800kg/h capacity, send me your material and capacity. I will suggest the suitable model.
```

## 另一个工业设备通用公式

```text
Don’t choose industrial machines only by price.

For B2B production, you need to check capacity, material suitability, motor, spare parts, delivery time, and after-sales support.

We provide testing video and packing proof before delivery.

Send your product and required capacity. I will help you choose the right model.
```

# 《文案圣经》外贸短视频主页私信转化文案应用笔记

## 一、这本书适合解决什么问题

《文案圣经》是一本非常适合转化场景的文案书。它的核心不是写漂亮句子，而是写能让客户行动的文案。

对我做外贸社媒获客来说，内容只是第一步，真正决定询盘质量和成交率的是每一个关键位置的文案：

- 视频开头3秒
- 视频字幕
- 封面标题
- 评论区回复
- 主页 Bio
- WhatsApp 第一段话
- 报价前说明
- 邮件标题
- CTA 行动号召
- 私信跟进

食品机械、工业设备不是冲动消费，客户不会因为一句夸张广告立刻下单。  
但好的文案可以让客户：

- 停下来
- 看明白
- 感觉和自己有关
- 相信你专业
- 愿意留言
- 愿意私信
- 愿意提供需求
- 愿意继续比较
- 愿意进入报价流程

《文案圣经》最大的价值是：它让我知道文案必须围绕客户利益、问题、证据和行动，而不是围绕我自己有多厉害。
