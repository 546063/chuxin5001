## 五、路由架构

```
用户问题
  │
  ├── 付费投流关键词 → paid_ads_router
  │     ├── Facebook → routing_rules_facebook_ads.json (14 routes)
  │     ├── TikTok → routing_rules_tiktok_ads.json (4 routes)
  │     ├── LinkedIn → routing_rules_linkedin_leads.json (3 routes)
  │     └── 巨量引擎 → routing_rules_douyin_juliang_ads.json (37 routes)
  │
  ├── 自然视频关键词 → organic_video_router
  │     ├── TikTok → routing_rules_overseas_video_leads.json (tiktok_organic)
  │     ├── YouTube Shorts → routing_rules_overseas_video_leads.json (youtube_shorts)
  │     ├── Instagram Reels → routing_rules_overseas_video_leads.json (instagram_reels)
  │     └── Facebook Reels → routing_rules_overseas_video_leads.json (facebook_reels)
  │
  ├── 国内视频号 → routing_rules_wechat_channels_leads.json (3 routes)
  │
  └── 跨平台对比 → 返回多个平台 entry_point
```

**付费 vs 自然判断规则**：

| 问题特征 | 判断 | 路由 |
|---------|------|------|
| 含"广告/投流/预算/CPL/ROAS" | 付费投流 | 对应平台付费路由 |
| 含"发视频/自然流量/免费/内容" | 自然获客 | 对应平台自然路由 |
| 含"组合/对比/选哪个" | 跨平台 | 多平台 entry_point |
