# 《谈判力》外贸原则谈判与跨文化成交应用笔记

## 一、这本书为什么适合外贸B2B谈判

《谈判力》来自哈佛谈判思路，核心是原则谈判。它不是教我如何压倒客户，而是教我如何在分歧中找到共同利益，用理性标准推动协议。

外贸B2B谈判里，客户和我经常有冲突：

- 客户想低价，我要合理利润
- 客户想赊销，我要控制风险
- 客户想快交货，工厂需要生产周期
- 客户想高配置低价格，我要保持方案合理
- 客户想先收货后付款，我要保护资金安全
- 客户想不断比较，我要推动决策

如果我只把谈判看成“谁让步更多”，最后很容易变成价格战。  
《谈判力》提醒我：谈判不是互相压价，而是双方一起解决问题。

对于中东、东南亚、非洲客户，原则谈判特别适合。因为这些地区的客户常常重视关系、议价和长期合作。如果我既尊重客户，又能坚持原则，就更容易建立长期信任。

---

## 二、核心模型/原则：5条

## 1. 把人和问题分开

客户砍价，不代表他坏。  
客户拖延，不代表他不认真。  
客户要求账期，不代表他想占便宜。  
客户对比竞争对手，不代表他不信任我。

谈判时，我不能把客户的要求理解成对我的攻击。  
我要把问题拿出来一起解决。

例如：

客户说：

```text
Your price is too high.
```

不要回：

```text
Our quality is better. You cannot compare.
```

更好：

```text
I understand price is important. Let’s compare the machine configuration and total value clearly, so you can make the right decision.
```

这样就把对立关系变成共同分析问题。

---

## 2. 关注利益，不只关注立场

立场是客户说出来的要求：

- cheaper price
- credit terms
- faster delivery
- free spare parts
- lower shipping cost
- longer warranty

利益是背后的真实原因：

- 控制预算
- 降低风险
- 方便内部审批
- 担心被骗
- 想提高利润
- 想保证售后
- 想赶上旺季
- 想给老板一个交代

如果我只回应立场，就会陷入讨价还价。  
如果我理解利益，就能找到替代方案。

例如客户要求赊销，背后可能是担心风险。  
我不能给赊销，但可以给：

- 测试视频
- 包装照片
- 发货文件
- 第三方验货
- 分阶段付款
- 老客户案例
- 视频会议验厂

这样既保护我，也回应客户的真实利益。

---

## 3. 创造多种选择，而不是只给一个答案

客户问价格时，如果我只有一个报价，客户只能说贵或不贵。  
如果我给多个方案，谈判空间就变大。

例如：

```text
Option A: lower-cost model
Option B: recommended model
Option C: higher-capacity model
```

客户要求更便宜时，我可以说：

```text
We can reduce cost by changing configuration, but I suggest you check whether the lower configuration can meet your production need.
```

客户要求快交货时，我可以给：

- 标准机更快
- 定制机更慢
- 分批发货
- 先发主机后发配件
- 当前库存型号替代

谈判力的核心是：不要卡在一个条件上，创造多个可接受方案。

---

## 4. 使用客观标准

谈判不能只靠“我觉得”和“你觉得”。

要引入客观标准：

- 实际产能
- 电机配置
- 刀片材质
- 不锈钢厚度
- 保修周期
- 交货周期
- 海运周期
- 包装方式
- 行业常见付款条款
- 机器测试流程
- 出口文件要求
- 过往客户案例

客户说别人便宜时，我用客观标准比较。  
客户要求账期时，我用行业常见首单付款规则说明。  
客户要求快交货时，我用生产、测试、包装和海运周期解释。

客观标准能让客户觉得我不是随便拒绝，而是有合理依据。

---

## 5. 明确自己的 BATNA

BATNA 是 Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement，意思是“谈不成时的最佳替代方案”。

简单说：我不能为了成交接受任何条件。  
我需要知道自己的底线：

- 最低可接受价格
- 是否接受账期
- 最低定金比例
- 是否接受定制取消
- 是否接受免费配件
- 是否接受特殊包装
- 是否接受低利润大单
- 是否值得继续跟进

如果客户要求超过底线，我要有礼貌地拒绝，而不是硬撑。

例如：

```text
For the first cooperation, we cannot offer credit terms. But we can support you with testing video, packing photos and shipment documents to reduce your risk.
```

有底线的销售，客户反而更尊重。

---

## 三、这本书解决我哪个成交卡点？

《谈判力》主要解决：

```text
客户有要求 → 双方有分歧 → 我不知道该坚持还是让步 → 谈判陷入价格战或僵局
```

具体包括：

- 客户砍价，我不知道如何回应
- 客户说别人便宜，我不知道如何比较
- 客户要求账期，我怕失去订单
- 客户大单拖很久，我不知道如何推进
- 客户不断增加要求，我没有边界
- 客户和我对交期、配置、付款有分歧
- 客户沟通强势，我容易被带节奏
- 客户文化不同，我不知道怎么既尊重又坚持原则

这本书最适合帮我建立“既合作又有边界”的谈判方式。

## 四、直接可用话术模板

## 1. 客户砍价：“能不能再便宜？”

## WhatsApp模板1：转向共同目标

```text
I understand you want a better price.

Our goal is the same: you need a machine that fits your production and avoids unnecessary risk.

Let’s check whether we can adjust the configuration or choose another model without affecting your main production need.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：给替代方案

```text
If the current model is beyond your budget, I can prepare a lower-cost option.

But I will also explain the difference in capacity, motor, blade and long-term use, so you can decide safely.
```

## 邮件模板

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your feedback.

I understand that budget is important. Instead of only reducing price, I suggest we compare different options and check which configuration can meet your production need.

We can prepare:
1. A lower-cost model
2. The recommended model
3. A higher-capacity model

This will help you choose based on both budget and machine suitability.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 2. 客户对比竞争对手价格

## WhatsApp模板1：用客观标准

```text
I understand.

To compare fairly, we need to check whether both offers include the same capacity, motor, blade material, stainless steel, warranty, testing video, packing and after-sales support.

If these points are different, the price comparison is not equal.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：不攻击竞争对手

```text
Another supplier may have a good offer. I don’t want to judge only by price.

What I suggest is comparing the key technical points and long-term use cost. Then you can choose the safer option for your factory.
```

## 邮件模板

```text
Hi [Name],

It is normal to compare suppliers.

To make the comparison clear, I suggest checking:
1. Machine configuration
2. Real working capacity
3. Material and blade quality
4. Testing before delivery
5. Warranty and spare parts
6. Delivery and packing details

If you want, I can help you compare our offer with the other option based on these objective points.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 3. 客户要求账期/赊销

## WhatsApp模板1：分开人和问题

```text
I understand your request.

For first cooperation, the issue is not trust only. The machine needs production, testing and packing before shipment, so we need a secure payment structure for both sides.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：提供替代风险控制

```text
We cannot offer credit terms for the first order.

But we can reduce your risk step by step: PI confirmation, production update, testing video before delivery, packing photos and shipment documents.
```

## 邮件模板

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your request.

For first cooperation, we normally use deposit before production and balance before shipment. This is because the machine needs to be produced, tested and packed according to the confirmed order.

To make the process safe for you, we can provide production updates, testing video, packing photos and shipment documents before delivery.

After stable cooperation is built, we can discuss more flexible terms for future orders.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 4. 大单客户决策周期太长

## WhatsApp模板1：推进小决策

```text
I understand this project needs more time.

To move forward step by step, can we first confirm the technical solution? After material, capacity, voltage and layout are clear, your team can discuss price and schedule more easily.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：给内部汇报材料

```text
Would it help if I prepare a summary for your team?

It can include machine list, capacity, key configuration, price options, delivery time and main differences. This may make internal discussion easier.
```

## 邮件模板

```text
Hi [Name],

I understand that this is a larger project and needs internal approval.

To support your decision process, I suggest we divide the project into three steps:

1. Confirm technical requirements
2. Compare machine options and budget
3. Finalize delivery schedule and payment plan

If needed, I can prepare a clear summary file for your team discussion.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## 五、一个可以立刻用的谈判/定价策略

## 策略：利益澄清四问

当客户提出任何要求时，不要马上答应或拒绝，先问清背后的利益。

## 四个问题

```text
1. Is your main concern budget, delivery time, or machine suitability?
2. Are you comparing with another supplier or checking your internal budget?
3. What is your target production capacity and expected start time?
4. If we adjust the solution, which point is most important for you: price, stability, delivery or after-sales support?
```

## 使用场景

客户说太贵：

```text
May I know your main concern is total budget or comparison with another supplier?
```

客户要求账期：

```text
May I know this request is mainly for cash flow arrangement or payment safety concern?
```

客户拖延：

```text
Is the delay mainly because of internal approval, budget, or technical confirmation?
```

先问清利益，再设计方案。  
这样我不会被客户表面要求牵着走。

## 六、一句话总结这本书对我成交的最大帮助

《谈判力》最大的帮助是：让我在跨文化B2B谈判中既保持合作态度，又守住价格、付款和交付底线，用共同利益和客观标准推动成交。
