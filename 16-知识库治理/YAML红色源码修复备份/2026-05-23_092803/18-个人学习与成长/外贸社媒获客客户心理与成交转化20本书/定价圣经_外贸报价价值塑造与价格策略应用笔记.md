# 《定价圣经》外贸报价价值塑造与价格策略应用笔记

## 一、这本书为什么适合外贸报价和促单

《定价圣经》的核心不是“怎么把价格报高”，而是告诉我：价格不是孤立数字，价格背后一定要有价值、定位、客户感知和交易结构。

对我做食品机械、工业设备外贸来说，客户在 WhatsApp、邮件、Facebook/TikTok/Instagram 评论区里经常会问：

- price?
- best price?
- any discount?
- why your price is higher?
- another supplier is cheaper
- can you give credit?
- can I pay after receiving goods?

如果我只围绕价格本身回应，就会被客户牵着走。  
客户问便宜，我就降价；客户说别人便宜，我就解释质量；客户说预算不够，我就让利。长期看，这会把自己做成低价供应商，利润越来越薄，客户质量也越来越差。

这本书对我的核心启发是：我不能只“报价”，我要“设计价格被理解的方式”。

对 B2B 机械设备来说，客户不是单纯买一台机器，而是在买：

- 产能
- 稳定性
- 人工替代
- 交付确定性
- 售后保障
- 生产风险降低
- 长期使用成本
- 供应商可信度

所以，我的报价必须从“机器多少钱”升级成“这套方案为什么值这个钱”。

---

## 二、核心模型/原则：5条

## 1. 价格必须绑定客户价值，而不是绑定成本

很多销售报价时只想着：

```text
成本 + 利润 = 报价
```

但客户不是按我的成本决定是否购买，而是按他感受到的价值决定是否下单。

对客户来说，一台切菜机的价值不是钢材、电机、刀片的成本，而是：

- 每天节省多少人工
- 切割速度提升多少
- 成品尺寸是否稳定
- 是否减少浪费
- 是否能赶上旺季
- 是否减少停机风险
- 是否适合本地原料
- 是否能长期稳定生产

所以报价前要先让客户看到“这台机器给他带来的业务价值”。

示例：

```text
This model is not only a vegetable cutter. For small food factories, it helps reduce manual cutting work, improve cutting consistency, and keep production more stable.
```

这句话先把价格从“买机器”变成“买生产能力”。

---

## 2. 不同客户应该有不同价格方案

不是所有客户都适合同一个方案。

客户可能分为：

- 小餐厅客户
- 小型食品工厂
- 中型加工厂
- 经销商
- 工程项目客户
- 第一次采购客户
- 老客户复购
- 大单客户
- 只比价客户
- 高度重视售后客户

如果我只有一个报价，客户只能选择“买或不买”。  
如果我设计多个方案，客户可以在我的价格体系内选择，而不是直接跑去找竞争对手。

例如：

```text
Option A: Basic model - lower cost, suitable for small capacity
Option B: Recommended model - better for stable factory production
Option C: High-capacity model - suitable for continuous production line
```

价格策略的关键是：不要让客户只和别人比价格，而是让客户在我的不同方案之间比较价值。

---

## 3. 低价不是优势，清晰的价值差异才是优势

外贸机械行业里，永远有人比我便宜。  
如果我跟着最低价走，就没有底线。

我必须让客户知道，价格差异来自哪里：

- 电机不同
- 刀片材质不同
- 实际产能不同
- 不锈钢厚度不同
- 包装方式不同
- 测试流程不同
- 售后支持不同
- 易损件配置不同
- 是否支持定制电压
- 是否有真实出货经验

客户说“别人更便宜”时，我不能只说“我们的质量好”。  
我要把价格拆成可比较项目。

示例：

```text
The price difference usually comes from real capacity, motor, blade material, stainless steel, packing, warranty and after-sales support. If we compare these points, the difference will be clearer.
```

---

## 4. 折扣不能随便给，必须换条件

价格一旦随便降，客户就会觉得：

- 原价有水分
- 还可以继续砍
- 你急着成交
- 你的价值不稳定

所以折扣不能是无条件让步。

可以交换：

- 更快确认订单
- 支付定金
- 增加采购数量
- 接受标准配置
- 减少定制要求
- 接受当前生产排期
- 选择更简单包装
- 后续复购承诺
- 成为案例客户

话术：

```text
I can check a better price for you, but it depends on order quantity and payment schedule. If you can confirm the model and arrange the deposit this week, I can apply for the best possible price.
```

核心：让步必须换确定性。

---

## 5. 客户买的是总成本，不是初始价格

B2B机械采购中，最低购买价不一定最低总成本。

低价机器可能带来：

- 产能不足
- 切割效果差
- 返工浪费
- 易坏
- 维修难
- 配件难买
- 停产损失
- 清洗不方便
- 售后没人管
- 二次采购成本

所以我要引导客户看 total cost of ownership，也就是“长期总成本”。

示例：

```text
For machinery, the cheapest purchase price may not mean the lowest total cost. If the machine cannot match your production, the cost of downtime, poor cutting result and replacement will be higher.
```

---

## 三、这本书解决我哪个成交卡点？

《定价圣经》主要解决我的报价卡点：

```text
客户只看价格 → 不理解价值 → 拿低价供应商压价 → 我被迫降价 → 利润变薄
```

它特别适合解决：

- 客户一上来只问 price
- 客户说 too expensive
- 客户拿竞争对手低价压我
- 客户觉得机器都差不多
- 客户不理解为什么价格更高
- 客户要求大幅折扣
- 客户只看采购价，不看长期使用成本
- 大单客户要求特殊价格
- 经销商想压低价格
- 我不知道如何解释报价

这本书提醒我：报价不是发送一个数字，而是建立一个客户愿意接受的价值框架。

## 四、直接可用话术模板

## 1. 客户砍价：“能不能再便宜？”

## WhatsApp模板1：不直接降价，先回到价值

```text
I understand you want a better price.

For this machine, I want to make sure we do not reduce the wrong part. The key value comes from real capacity, motor, blade material, machine stability and after-sales support.

If your budget is limited, I can prepare a lower-cost option, and explain the difference clearly.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：折扣换条件

```text
I can check the best possible price for you.

But it depends on order quantity and payment schedule. If you can confirm the model and arrange the deposit this week, I can try to apply for a better price and keep the production schedule for you.
```

## 邮件模板

```text
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your feedback.

I understand that price is important. For this machine, the cost is related to motor, blade material, real working capacity, stainless steel, packing and after-sales support.

If you want a lower-cost option, we can prepare another model for comparison. However, I suggest comparing not only the purchase price, but also long-term use, spare parts, warranty and machine suitability.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## 2. 客户对比竞争对手价格

## WhatsApp模板1：要求公平比较

```text
I understand another supplier may offer a lower price.

To compare fairly, please check whether the machine configuration is the same: real capacity, motor, blade material, stainless steel, warranty, spare parts and packing.

If these are different, the price will naturally be different.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：让客户发送对方规格

```text
If you can send me the basic specifications of the other offer, I can help you compare the difference clearly.

Sometimes two machines look similar from outside, but the motor, blade, material and real capacity are different.
```

## 邮件模板

```text
Hi [Name],

Different prices usually come from different configurations and service levels.

When comparing offers, I suggest checking:
1. Real working capacity
2. Motor and blade quality
3. Stainless steel material
4. Testing before delivery
5. Spare parts and warranty
6. Packing and after-sales support

This will help you compare the total value, not only the first price.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## WhatsApp模板1：礼貌拒绝赊销

```text
I understand your request.

For first cooperation, we usually cannot offer credit terms because the machine needs production, testing and packing before shipment.

We can support you with clear PI, testing video before delivery, packing photos and shipment documents to reduce your risk step by step.
```

## WhatsApp模板2：给替代方案

```text
For the first order, our normal payment term is deposit before production and balance before shipment.

After we build stable cooperation and order history, we can discuss more flexible terms for future orders.
```

```text

For first-time cooperation, we normally work with deposit before production and balance before shipment. Since the machine is produced and tested according to the confirmed order, we cannot offer credit terms for the first order.

To reduce your purchasing risk, we will provide testing video, packing photos and shipment documents before delivery.

After several successful orders, we can discuss more flexible cooperation terms.
```

```text
I understand this is a big decision.

To make it easier, we can first confirm the technical solution: material, capacity, voltage, layout and machine list. After the solution is clear, price and delivery time will be easier to decide.
```

## 五、一个可以立刻用的谈判/定价策略

## 策略：三档报价法

不要只给客户一个价格。  
给客户三个方案：

```text
Basic Option 基础方案
- 价格低
- 适合小产能/试用/预算敏感客户
- 配置标准
- 售后基础

Recommended Option 推荐方案
- 性价比最高
- 适合稳定生产
- 配置更稳
- 产能更适合工厂
- 推荐客户优先选择

Premium Option 高配方案
- 适合连续生产/大产能
- 更强配置
- 更长时间运行
- 更完整配套
```

报价表达：

```text
I prepared 3 options for you:

Option A is lower cost, suitable for small capacity.
Option B is recommended for stable factory production.
Option C is for higher capacity and long-term continuous use.

Most factory customers choose Option B because it balances price, capacity and stability.
```

这个策略的好处：

- 客户不再只问“能不能便宜”
- 客户开始在我的方案内比较
- 推荐方案更容易成交
- 低价方案可以承接预算客户
- 高配方案可以拉高价值锚点

---

## 六、一句话总结这本书对我成交的最大帮助

《定价圣经》最大的帮助是：让我把报价从“一个数字”变成“价值、配置、风险和长期成本的解释系统”，让客户觉得贵有贵的理由、值有值的证据。
