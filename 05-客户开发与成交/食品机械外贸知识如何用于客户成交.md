# 食品机械外贸知识如何用于客户成交

## 一句话总结

外贸基础知识是成交的核心工具——帮助准确报价、解答客户疑虑、谈判更好的付款方式、建立信任、降低收款风险。

---

## 成交各阶段的应用

### 阶段 1：询价与报价

**外贸知识应用**：

| 客户问 | 知识应用 | 操作 |
|--------|----------|------|
| "价格是多少？" | 确认贸易术语 | 先问 FOB/CIF/DDP？ |
| "有CE认证吗？" | 提供认证文件 | 发送 CE 证书 |
| "最小订购量是多少？" | 说明 MOQ | 解释 MOQ 影响价格 |
| "交货期多久？" | 说明 Lead Time | 给出完整时间线 |

**报价标准流程**：

```
Step 1: 确认客户需求（设备型号、数量、目的港）
Step 2: 选择贸易术语（FOB/CIF/DDP）
Step 3: 计算价格（设备成本 + 运费 + 保险 + 清关）
Step 4: 说明付款方式（T/T 30/70）
Step 5: 提供 PI（包含所有信息）
```

---

### 阶段 2：谈判与议价

**外贸知识应用**：

| 客户压价 | 知识应对 |
|----------|----------|
| "你的价格太贵了" | 解释 FOB vs CIF 差异，让客户知道总价构成 |
| "能不能OA 60天？" | 解释为什么新人首单要用 T/T 30/70 |
| "要打折" | 解释 MOQ 和批量价格差异 |
| "运费太高" | 解释 FCL vs LCL，给出不同方案 |

**谈判话术示例**：

**客户嫌价格高**：
```
I understand price is important. Let me explain our pricing:

FOB Shanghai USD 2,800 includes:
✅ Machine cost (quality materials, CE certified)
✅ Export customs clearance
✅ Delivery to Shanghai port

Not included (for your budget planning):
❌ Ocean freight: ~USD 500-800
❌ Insurance: ~USD 100
❌ Your customs clearance: ~USD 150
❌ Transportation to your factory: ~USD 100

CIF Veracruz would be USD 3,450 (all included).

Comparing with other suppliers, make sure they're quoting the same terms!
```

---

### 阶段 3：合同与付款

**外贸知识应用**：

| 环节 | 知识应用 | 操作 |
|------|----------|------|
| 合同条款 | 明确贸易术语 | 在 PI/Sales Contract 写明 |
| 付款方式 | 选择安全的 T/T 30/70 | 解释为什么最安全 |
| 风险控制 | 识别高风险客户 | 用外贸知识判断 |
| 收款保障 | 确保定金到账再生产 | 财务控制 |

**标准合同要素**：

```markdown
Product: MS-2000 Meat Slicer
Quantity: 3 units
Unit Price: USD 2,800 FOB Shanghai
Total: USD 8,400

Terms:
- Trade Term: FOB Shanghai
- Payment: T/T 30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment
- Lead Time: 25 days after deposit received
- Package: Wooden crate with ISPM 15 certification
- Documents: PI, BL copy, CE Certificate, Packing List
```

---

### 阶段 4：发货与收款

**外贸知识应用**：

| 环节 | 知识应用 | 操作 |
|------|----------|------|
| 生产跟进 | 发送生产照片/视频 | 让客户放心 |
| 发货准备 | 确认所有单据齐全 | 发票、装箱单、提单、认证 |
| 发货通知 | 发送 BL copy + 发货照片 | 让客户准备清关 |
| 收款确认 | 确认尾款到账 | 再放 BL 正本 |
| 交付 | 发货后跟进客户收货 | 确保满意 |

## 成交话术示例

### 报价时

**客户问价**：
```
Thank you for your inquiry about the MS-2000 meat slicer!

To give you an accurate quote, I need to know:

1. Destination port? (Veracruz, Hamburg, Los Angeles?)
2. Trade terms preferred? (FOB Shanghai / CIF your port / DDP?)
3. Order quantity? (MOQ is 3 units)

This helps me calculate the exact total cost for you, including shipping and insurance.

What are your requirements?
```

### 谈判时

**客户要求 OA**：
```
I understand you'd prefer Open Account terms. For building a long-term partnership, we'd love to offer better payment terms.

However, for the first order, we recommend T/T 30/70:
- 30% deposit confirms your order and starts production
- 70% balance is paid before we ship

This protects both sides:
- For you: We won't ship until payment is confirmed
- For us: We have commitment to start production

After 2-3 successful orders, we can discuss OA terms for you.

Does T/T 30/70 work for now?
```
