# LinkedIn DM话术库

> **当前状态**：参考级话术库，基于行业经验，待真实运营后验证。

---

## 一、连接请求话术

### 模板 1：行业关联
```
Hi [Name], I see you're in the food processing industry. 
We help food manufacturers increase production efficiency 
with automated equipment. Would love to connect!
```

### 模板 2：地域关联
```
Hi [Name], we recently exported several machines to [country]. 
I'd love to connect with professionals in your market.
```

### 模板 3：内容关联
```
Hi [Name], great post about [topic]! I work in food machinery 
and have some insights on this. Would love to connect.
```

---

## 二、首次 DM 话术

### 模板 1：产品介绍型
```
Hi [Name], thanks for connecting!

We're a food machinery manufacturer specializing in:
🥟 Dumpling/Baozi machines
🥬 Vegetable processing lines
🍖 Meat processing equipment

15+ years experience, exported to 50+ countries.
If you ever need to upgrade your production line, 
happy to send a free quote!

Best, [Name]
```

### 模板 2：价值提供型
```
Hi [Name], thanks for the connection!

I noticed you're managing production at [Company]. 
We recently helped a similar company reduce labor costs 
by 60% with automated food processing equipment.

Would you be interested in a quick 5-min chat about 
how we did it? No pressure at all.

Best, [Name]
```

## 三、跟进话术

### 跟进 1（7 天后，无回复）
```
Hi [Name], just wanted to share a quick case study 
about how we helped a food processor in [country] 
scale their production from 50kg/hour to 500kg/hour.

[Link]

Thought it might be relevant. Let me know if you'd 
like to discuss your production needs!
```

### 跟进 2（14 天后，无回复）
```
Hi [Name], last follow-up from me. 

We're currently offering free production line audits 
for food manufacturers. If that's something you'd 
find useful, just let me know.

Either way, great to be connected!
```

### 跟进 3（有回复但无需求）
```
Thanks for the reply, [Name]! No problem at all.

I'll keep you in our network. If you ever need 
food processing equipment or know someone who does, 
feel free to reach out.

Have a great week!
```

## 四、报价引导话术

### 收到询盘后
```
Great to hear from you, [Name]!

To give you the most accurate quote, could you share:
1. What product(s) you want to process?
2. Desired capacity (kg/hour)?
3. Any specific requirements?

I'll prepare a detailed proposal within 24 hours.

Best, [Name]
[Title] | [Company]
📧 [Email] | 📱 [WhatsApp]
```

### 发送报价后
```
Hi [Name], attached is the detailed proposal for 
[machine name]. It includes:

✅ Technical specifications
✅ Pricing (FOB/CIF options)
✅ Delivery timeline
✅ Warranty & after-sales terms

Happy to jump on a call to discuss any questions. 
Available this week?

Best, [Name]
```

## 五、注意事项

| 注意 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 个性化 | 每条消息提到对方公司/行业 |
| 简短 | DM 不超过 100 字 |
| 不硬广 | 先建立关系再推销 |
| 有节奏 | 不要频繁跟进（7-14 天间隔） |
| 提供价值 | 分享行业洞察/案例 |
