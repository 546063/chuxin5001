## 二、核心理论：5个关键原则

## 1. 说话先看场景，不同场景用不同表达目标

同一句话，在不同场景下作用不同。

客户刚看完视频来问价，我的目标不是马上成交，而是让他提供需求。  
客户已经收到报价，我的目标不是重复介绍产品，而是确认顾虑。  
客户说价格贵，我的目标不是争论，而是重建价值框架。  
客户犹豫不回复，我的目标不是催，而是降低回复门槛。

所以每条消息发出前，都要先判断：

```text
当前沟通处于哪个阶段？
我的目标是吸引、解释、确认、安抚、比较，还是促单？
```

外贸沟通不能所有客户都用同一句：

```text
Please contact me.
Please check quotation.
Any update?
```

这类话太粗糙。

---

## 2. 先接住对方，再表达自己

好的沟通不是马上输出观点，而是先让客户感觉被理解。

客户说：

“Too expensive.”

不要马上说：

“Our quality is good.”

更好的方式是：

```text
I understand. Price is important for machinery purchase.
```

然后再表达：

```text
For this model, the price difference mainly comes from motor, blade, real capacity, packing and after-sales support.
```

先接住客户，客户才愿意继续听。

在中东、东南亚、非洲客户沟通里，这点很重要。客户未必英语很好，也未必愿意长篇解释，如果我一上来就反驳，很容易让对方沉默。

---

## 3. 把复杂问题说简单

食品机械和工业设备有很多技术细节：

- 电压
- 产能
- 材质
- 电机
- 刀片
- 原料适配
- 生产线搭配
- 清洗维护
- 包装运输
- 售后配件

客户不一定懂这些。  
我不能把一堆参数直接丢给客户，而要用客户能理解的方式说。

比如：

复杂表达：

```text
The blade structure and motor power vary according to processing material and expected throughput.
```

更适合 WhatsApp 的表达：

```text
Different materials need different blades and motor power. That is why I need to confirm what you process and how many kg per hour you need.
```

简单不是低级，而是降低客户理解成本。

---

## 4. 好表达要给客户台阶

客户说错了、误解了、压价了、犹豫了，都不要让客户难堪。

比如客户说：

“Your machine is same as other supplier but more expensive.”

不要说：

```text
No, not same.
You don’t understand quality.
```

更好的表达：

```text
I understand it may look similar from the outside. But the difference is usually in motor, blade material, real capacity, stainless steel thickness, warranty and after-sales support.
```

这句话给客户台阶：  
他不是不懂，只是“从外观看起来像”。  
然后我再补充专业信息。

这类表达对中东客户尤其重要，因为尊重和面子会影响后续谈判氛围。

---

## 5. 每次沟通都要留下下一步

好好说话不是说得好听，而是说完后能推进。

每次沟通最好带一个低压力、具体的下一步：

- Please tell me your material.
- Could you confirm your required capacity?
- Would you like a lower-cost option?
- Can I send you the testing video?
- Do you want me to compare two models?
- Should I prepare the quotation with shipping cost?
- Would you like to check the packing photo?

不要让对话停在空泛的“ok”“thanks”“any update”。

---

## 三、这本书帮我解决什么阶段的问题？

## 主要解决阶段

```text
吸引关注 → 建立信任 → 推动询盘 → 促成下单
```

《好好说话》不是只适合某一个阶段，而是贯穿整个社媒获客转化链路。

具体解决：

- 视频文案太像广告，客户不想看
- 评论区回复太生硬，客户不进私信
- 主页简介太空泛，客户不知道我能帮什么
- 私信开场太急，客户不愿意提供需求
- 报价后跟进太像催单，客户不回复
- 客户说贵时，我容易解释过度或降价
- 客户再考虑时，我不知道怎么自然推进
- 客户误解价格和配置差异时，我不知道怎么说清楚
- 客户沉默时，我不知道怎么唤醒

这本书对我的价值是：让每个转化节点的话，都更顺、更清楚、更能推进。

## 四、直接可用的5个具体应用方法

## 1. 短视频内容设计：用“客户听得懂的话”讲机器

视频不要只讲参数，要讲客户能理解的业务结果。

## 视频结构

```text
客户问题
↓
简单解释原因
↓
机器解决方案
↓
真实画面证明
↓
引导客户询问
```

## 示例脚本

```text
Still cutting onions by hand in your food factory?

Manual cutting is slow, and the size is not always stable.

This vegetable cutting machine can help small factories cut onion, potato and carrot faster with more stable size.

Before delivery, we test the machine with real materials.

Send me your material and capacity, I will suggest the suitable model.
```

## 适合标题

- “Don’t choose food machines only by price”
- “Tell me your material, I tell you the suitable machine”
- “For small food factories: how to reduce manual cutting work”
- “Why does the same machine have different prices?”
- “Before buying, check material, capacity and voltage”

---

## 2. 主页/简介文案：一眼说清楚“你帮谁解决什么问题”

主页简介要避免空话。

错误：

```text
Professional machinery manufacturer with high quality and good service.
```

更好：

```text
Food Processing Machine Supplier
Helping small factories choose suitable machines
Vegetable | Meat | Dough | Washing | Cutting Lines
Send material + capacity for model suggestion
WhatsApp: [number]
```

## 面向中东、东南亚、非洲版本

```text
Food Processing Machines for B2B Buyers
Middle East | Southeast Asia | Africa
Testing video before delivery
Machine suggestion by material + capacity + voltage
DM / WhatsApp for quotation
```

这个简介把“我是谁、帮谁、怎么帮、下一步做什么”讲清楚了。

---

## 3. 私信开场白：低压力提问，让客户容易回复

私信第一句话不要太长。  
目标是让客户回复一个简单信息。

## 客户问 price

```text
Thanks for your inquiry. I can send the price.

Please tell me your material and required kg/h first, so I can quote the correct model.
```

## 客户说 interested

```text
Thanks. May I know what material you process? Potato, onion, cabbage, meat or dough?
```

## 客户问 machine details

```text
Sure. This machine has different models. Is it for restaurant use, small factory, or continuous production?
```

## 客户问 shipping

```text
Yes, we can arrange shipping. Please tell me your country/city and local voltage, then I can check the solution.
```

这些开场白都很短，但能让客户继续说下去。

---

## 4. 报价后的跟进话术：把“催客户”改成“帮客户比较”

## 错误跟进

```text
Any update?
Please check.
Do you want to order?
```

## 正确跟进1：确认顾虑

```text
Hi [Name], I’m following up on the quotation.

May I know if your main concern is price, delivery time, or machine suitability? Then I can send the right information.
```

## 正确跟进2：补充证据

```text
I’m also sending the testing video again for your reference. Please check the cutting result and machine stability. These two points are important for long-term use.
```

## 正确跟进3：给选择

```text
If the recommended model is beyond your budget, I can prepare a lower-cost option and explain the difference clearly.
```

报价后的跟进不能只是提醒，而要帮助客户降低决策难度。

---

## 5. 客户犹豫时的应对：先理解，再分解问题

## 客户说价格太贵

```text
I understand. Price is important.

For this machine, the cost difference usually comes from motor, blade, real capacity, material, packing and after-sales support.

If your budget is limited, I can also prepare a lower-cost option for comparison.
```

## 客户说再考虑

```text
Sure, no problem.

To help you compare more easily, may I know what you are considering most now: price, quality, delivery time, or after-sales support?
```

## 客户说别人更便宜

```text
I understand. From the outside, some machines may look similar.

But the real difference is usually in motor, blade, capacity, stainless steel, warranty and spare parts support. If you want, I can help you compare the key points.
```

这类话术的共同点是：不硬顶、不自降身份、不攻击对方，而是把对话带回专业比较。

---

## 五、针对中东B2B客户的特别注意事项

## 1. 语气要尊重，避免命令感

少用：

```text
You must confirm today.
You should pay now.
You need to decide fast.
```

多用：

```text
I suggest...
It may be better to...
To avoid delay, we can...
For your production plan, I recommend...
```

中东客户更容易接受建议式表达，而不是命令式表达。

---

## 2. 适度关系化，但不要油腻

可以使用：

```text
my friend
brother
thanks for your trust
I understand your concern
we can check together
```

但不要过度使用。  
真正建立信任的不是称呼，而是专业、证据和持续服务。

---

## 3. 议价时保持体面

中东客户议价常见。不要因为客户砍价就觉得对方不专业。

可以这样说：

```text
I understand you need a better price.

Let me check what we can do based on the machine configuration and order plan. But I also want to make sure the machine can meet your production need.
```

这既给客户面子，也保住专业立场。

---

## 4. 解释价格时要结构化

不要只说“quality better”。

应该说：

```text
The price difference mainly comes from:
1. Motor
2. Blade material
3. Real capacity
4. Stainless steel
5. Packing
6. Warranty and spare parts support
```

结构化表达比情绪化解释更可信。

---

## 5. 客户不回复时，给他轻松回来的台阶

不要说：

```text
Why no reply?
Are you still interested?
```

更好：

```text
Hi [Name], no problem if the project is delayed.

If you still need this machine later, just send me your material and capacity. I can continue helping you choose the suitable model.
```

这句话让客户不尴尬，后面还有机会回来。

---

## 六、一句话总结这本书对我的最大价值

《好好说话》最大的价值是：让我在短视频、主页、评论、私信、报价和跟进中，把每句话都说得更清楚、更尊重、更能推动客户进入下一步。

# 《好好说话》外贸社媒文字沟通与高转化表达应用笔记

## 一、这本书为什么适合外贸社媒转化

《好好说话》强调的是不同沟通场景下如何表达：怎么说服、怎么谈判、怎么沟通、怎么表达观点、怎么避免误解。

对我做外贸社媒获客来说，这本书非常实用，因为我的成交过程大量依赖文字沟通：

- 视频字幕
- 评论区回复
- 主页简介
- Facebook 私信
- Instagram 私信
- WhatsApp 聊天
- 邮件报价
- 报价后跟进
- 客户异议处理
- 促单话术

非面对面沟通最大的风险是：客户看不到我的表情和语气，只能通过文字判断我是否专业、是否尊重他、是否值得信任。

很多外贸销售失败，不是因为产品不好，而是因为表达方式让客户觉得：

- 你太急
- 你太硬
- 你不懂我
- 你只想让我付款
- 你没有回答重点
- 你不像专业供应商
- 你没有给我安全感

《好好说话》的价值是：让我把“会聊天”变成“能转化的表达能力”。
